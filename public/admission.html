Admission Process

Admissions are open for various engineering programs. Students must fulfill the eligibility criteria and follow the admission procedure as specified by the institute.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Candidate must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics.
  • Minimum 45% marks in qualifying examination.
  • Admission may be based on entrance examination or merit.

Required Documents

  • 10th Marksheet
  • 12th Marksheet
  • Entrance Exam Scorecard
  • Passport Size Photographs
  • Aadhar Card
