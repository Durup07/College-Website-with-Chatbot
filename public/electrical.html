About the Department

The Electrical Engineering Department at DAV Institute of Engineering & Technology focuses on providing strong theoretical and practical knowledge in electrical systems, power generation, control systems, and electrical machines. The department prepares students for careers in power industries, automation, renewable energy and electrical design.

Students are trained through laboratory work, industrial visits, workshops, and real-world projects to enhance their technical skills and innovation.
